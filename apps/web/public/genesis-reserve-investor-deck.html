    Legal Structure
    Genesis Reserve Entity Architecture
    Genesis Holdings, LLC
    Wyoming LLC · Holding Entity
    Top-level holding company. Owns equity in all downstream entities. Provides asset isolation for investor capital and IP.
    Genesis App, Inc.
    Delaware C-Corp · Investor Equity
    Issues preferred stock to investors. Holds all IP, technology, and employee contracts. Delaware Court of Chancery governs.
    Genesis Reserve Trust
    Wyoming Statutory Trust · Member Custodian
    Holds member deposits. Members are beneficial owners. Capital is not on Genesis Reserve's balance sheet.
    Genesis IMO, LLC
    Georgia LLC · Insurance · Year 2
    Licensed insurance marketing organization. Issues and manages YFLPP policies. Carrier relationships and agent commissions.
    Why Delaware C-Corp for Investment
    Investor-Standard Structure
    Every institutional investor and VC expects Delaware C-Corp for preferred stock issuance. Delaware Court of Chancery is the gold standard for corporate dispute resolution. SAFEs convert into preferred equity in this entity.
    Why Wyoming Statutory Trust for Members
    Members Own Their Capital
    The Trust holds member deposits — not Genesis Reserve. Members are beneficial owners. This is the structural trust signal that makes Genesis Reserve credible to communities historically burned by financial institutions.
    Phase 2 — Year 2
    Georgia IMO · Insurance Rail
    Georgia-licensed IMO entity enables YFLPP at full scale. Phase 1 launches via IMO partnership (30–60 days). Phase 2 brings the licensing in-house as agent network justifies it.

    Business Model
    Multiple revenue streams. All from a single member relationship.
    Stream 1
    Yield Spread
    Genesis Reserve earns a spread between gross protocol yield and net yield distributed to members. As total deposit volume grows, spread compounds.
    Model: AUM-based · Scales with deposit growth

    YFLPP Premium Management
    Fee on yield routed through YFLPP. As more members activate insurance funding, recurring premium volume generates a durable, recurring revenue line.
