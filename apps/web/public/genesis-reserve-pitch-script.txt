PRACTICE TIPS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Use the deck's built-in countdown timer. Press ▶ to start autoplay.
  The badge counts 20→1. Finish your line before it hits zero.

• If you run long on a slide, cut the last sentence — the core message
  is always in the first two lines.

• Slides 08–11 (the phone demos) are your hero section. Slow down here.
  The audience is watching the screen — let the visual breathe.

• Slide 15 (The Ask) has the most words by design — it's the close.
  Practice it until it feels conversational, not recited.

• On Slide 02 (Team), make eye contact when you say "I'm Anthony Beedles."
  That's the credibility moment. Own it.

• The three quotes (Slides 03, 04, 06) are written to land like gut punches.
  Pause one beat after delivering each one.
