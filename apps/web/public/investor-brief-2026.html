01 Executive Summary

Genesis Reserve is a self-custody DeFi wallet built on Arbitrum One that automatically routes user deposits into institutional-grade yield strategies — Aave V3, Balancer V3, and T-Bill equivalents — delivering real returns every 15 minutes. The product is positioned as the Smarter Wallet: an alternative to savings accounts and money market funds for people who refuse to stay on the wrong side of financial history.

The core infrastructure is built and deployed. Smart contracts are live on Arbitrum One mainnet. The web application handles authentication (Privy embedded wallets), vault deposits and withdrawals, fiat on/off ramp (Transak + Ramp Network), a compliant card payout system (Stripe), and a live crypto news intelligence feed. The product is functionally complete at an MVP level.

This raise is a targeted $50K–$100K seed to close the final engineering gaps, complete a smart contract security audit, establish legal infrastructure, and execute a 100-user paid beta — the proof-of-traction milestone required to raise a formal seed round at scale.

The ask is not to build the product. The product is built. This capital closes the gap between a working MVP and a production-grade system with real users, real money, and real data to bring to the next table.
