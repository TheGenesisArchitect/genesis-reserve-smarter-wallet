Legal Infrastructure
Genesis Reserve Entity Architecture

Genesis Reserve is structured as a four-entity stack. This isolates liability, optimizes tax treatment at each layer, satisfies investor expectations, and positions the company for regulated financial services expansion — including the insurance distribution arm — without cross-contaminating the core technology company.

Genesis Reserve Holdings, LLC
Wyoming · Parent Holding Company
Founders hold equity here via AMB Trust structure. Owns all subsidiary interests. No state income tax. Wyoming Digital Asset laws (HB0070) are the strongest in the US.
Genesis App, Inc.
Delaware C-Corp · Operating Entity
Issues preferred stock to investors. Holds all IP, technology, and employee contracts. Investor standard — Delaware Court of Chancery governs.
Genesis Reserve IMO, LLC
Georgia · Insurance Distribution · Phase 2
Yield Funded Legacy Protection Program distribution arm. Isolates insurance regulatory requirements. Georgia domicile for initial agent licensing.
Genesis Reserve Trust, LLC
Wyoming Statutory Trust · Member Custodian
Holds member yield protocol positions and stablecoin reserves. Operates under Wyoming Trust Act (SF0125). Evolves into state-chartered trust company. Makes member assets legally protected and custodied.
Why This Stack
Wyoming gives us the most progressive Digital Asset law in the country. Delaware gives investors what they expect. The Trust entity gives members legal assurance their assets are custodied — not commingled. The IMO entity keeps insurance regulatory requirements from touching the core tech company. This is the architecture that gets institutional investors to the table.
