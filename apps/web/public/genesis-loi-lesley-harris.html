Non-Binding Letter of Intent

Seed Round — Lead Investment Position

This Letter of Intent (this "LOI") is submitted as of May 30, 2026 (the "LOI Date"), by and between:

Genesis App, Inc., a Delaware corporation, doing business as Genesis Reserve, with its principal place of business at 218 Neely Rd, Fayetteville, GA 30214 (the "Company"); and

Lesley Harris, an individual (the "Investor" and, together with the Company, the "Parties").

This LOI sets forth the principal terms and conditions under which the Investor intends to make an investment in the Company as part of the Company's initial seed financing round (the "Seed Round"). Except as expressly set forth in Sections 7 (Exclusivity), 8 (Confidentiality), 9 (Governing Law), and 10 (Expenses), this LOI is non-binding and does not create any legal obligation on either Party.

Non-Binding Notice. This Letter of Intent is a statement of intent only. The proposed terms set forth herein are subject to negotiation, due diligence, and execution of definitive agreements. No binding obligation to invest, close, or proceed shall arise until both Parties have executed a definitive SAFE Agreement (as defined herein). The only binding provisions of this LOI are Sections 7, 8, 9, and 10.

1. Background & Purpose

Genesis Reserve is a digital-native financial infrastructure platform providing members access to on-chain yield, custodial treasury services, and a Smarter Wallet interface designed for responsible wealth accumulation. The Company is a top-performing graduate of its accelerator cohort, graduating June 12, 2026, in Berlin, Germany.

The Company is raising a $500,000 Seed Round via Post-Money Simple Agreements for Future Equity (SAFEs), structured across up to five (5) investor seats. The Investor has agreed to serve as Lead Investor and Lead Advisor for the Seed Round. This LOI reflects the proposed terms of the Investor's participation.

3. Lead Investor Rights & Designations

In recognition of the Investor's role as Lead Investor and Lead Advisor, and subject to execution of the definitive SAFE and a separate Advisor Agreement, the Company agrees to extend the following rights and designations:

Right / Designation Terms
Lead Investor Designation The Investor shall be designated as the Lead Investor for the Seed Round. All other Seed Round investors will be acknowledged as co-investors under the Lead Investor's referral relationship.
Board Observer Seat Non-voting board observer seat for the duration of the investment. The Investor shall receive all board meeting notices, agendas, and materials delivered to board members, and shall have the right to attend and participate (but not vote) at all board meetings.
Information Rights Quarterly financial statements, product metrics, and investor updates. Annual audited financials (when available). Prompt notice of any material events, regulatory developments, or round activity.
Advisory Equity 0.75% of the Company's fully diluted capitalization, issued pursuant to a separate Advisor Agreement. Vesting schedule: 4-year straight-line vest with a 1-year cliff. Acceleration: single-trigger acceleration upon a Change of Control.
Security Audit Engagement The Company engages the Investor to conduct a paid penetration testing and SOC compliance framework engagement, commencing during Weeks 5–7 following Seed Round close. Scope, compensation, and deliverables to be set forth in a separate Statement of Work.
Seat Referral Priority The Investor shall have first right to introduce candidates for any remaining Seed Round seats. Any investor introduced by the Investor and admitted to the round will be attributed to the Investor's referral relationship and noted in Company records accordingly.
Lead Investor Designation — Future Rounds The Company agrees in good faith to acknowledge the Investor's lead contribution in all future investor materials and communications, and to provide the Investor with advance notice of any Series A process initiation no less than thirty (30) days prior to any public announcement or outreach to new investors.

4. Conditions to Closing

The closing of the investment contemplated by this LOI is subject to the satisfaction or waiver of the following conditions:

  1. Execution of a definitive Post-Money SAFE Agreement in a form mutually acceptable to the Parties;
  2. Execution of a separate Advisor Agreement setting forth advisory equity vesting terms;
  3. Completion of entity formation (Wyoming Holdings LLC, Wyoming Statutory Trust, and any related subsidiary entities) to the Company's reasonable satisfaction;
  4. Completion of Investor's due diligence review of the Company's data room materials, technical architecture, cap table, and financial projections;
  5. Receipt of the Investment Amount by wire transfer to the Company's designated account; and
  6. No material adverse change in the Company's business, financial condition, or technology between the LOI Date and the closing date.

5. Pre-Closing Covenants

From the LOI Date through the earlier of closing or termination of this LOI, the Company agrees to:

  1. Provide the Investor with access to the Company's full data room, including financial models, technical architecture documentation, cap table, and the 30-Day Operational Blueprint;
  2. Promptly respond to any reasonable due diligence requests from the Investor;
  3. Notify the Investor of any material adverse change in the Company's business or any new investor discussions; and
  4. Not issue any equity, SAFEs, convertible notes, or other securities to any third party without first notifying the Investor, except as otherwise agreed in writing.

6. No Obligation to Proceed

Nothing in this LOI obligates either Party to execute any definitive agreement, consummate any investment, or proceed with any transaction described herein. Either Party may terminate this LOI at any time upon written notice to the other Party, subject only to the surviving obligations set forth in Sections 7 through 10.

7. Exclusivity [Binding]

From the LOI Date through the date that is thirty (30) days thereafter (the "Exclusivity Period"), the Company agrees not to solicit, negotiate, or accept any investment from any third party in the Seed Round without first providing the Investor with written notice and a 5-business-day right of first opportunity to match or improve any such proposed terms. This Section 7 is binding upon execution of this LOI.

8. Confidentiality [Binding]

The existence of this LOI, its terms, and all information disclosed in connection with the transactions contemplated herein are Confidential Information subject to the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement between Genesis App, Inc. and Lesley Harris, effective May 12, 2026 (the "NDA"), which is incorporated herein by reference. The Parties' obligations under the NDA shall govern all confidentiality matters and shall survive any termination of this LOI. This Section 8 is binding upon execution of this LOI.

9. Governing Law [Binding]

The binding provisions of this LOI (Sections 7 through 10) shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Any dispute arising out of or relating to the binding provisions of this LOI shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in New Castle County, Delaware. This Section 9 is binding upon execution of this LOI.

10. Expenses [Binding]

Each Party shall bear its own legal fees, costs, and expenses incurred in connection with the negotiation, preparation, and execution of this LOI and any definitive agreements contemplated herein, unless otherwise agreed in writing. This Section 10 is binding upon execution of this LOI.

11. Expiration

This LOI shall expire and be of no further force or effect if not executed by both Parties on or before June 10, 2026, unless extended by mutual written agreement.
